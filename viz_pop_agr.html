POPULATION CHANGE

Population growth is reshaping the planet at an unprecedented scale. As the number of people increases, the land and resources required to sustain life are placed under growing pressure. The purpose of this interactive map is to explore the dinamics between global population growth and changes in cropland cover betwwen the year 1990 and 2023, enabling users to examine how demographic dynamics and agricultural land use evolve across different regions of the world.
